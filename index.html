Welcome to my travel website!

Why do people love to travel so much? It would seem that in the age of information technology you don’t even have to leave your home. Thus, you can see the sights of the world on your computer screen, and with the help of modern technologies you can even feel the effect of being in a certain country.

But only about the person who travels can we say that he is truly rich. After all, he not only visits different countries and cultures, but also changes himself, transforms, opens up to new things and learns about the world.

Why travel?

Most of our life is spent at work, family or household matters. Looking back, we realize that we spent a lot of time trying to earn money, get an education, and raise children. And if you don’t change your environment and constantly be in the same type of atmosphere, you can lose yourself and lose interest in life.

Traveling helps us take a break from everyday life, forget about work, shake off stress and problems, and reevaluate our lives. By visiting other countries or cities, relaxing at resorts, splashing in the warm sea, we appreciate the world in a new way, and also get to know ourselves better. Only when traveling is it possible to completely relax, open up and organize your thoughts. And by breaking out of the routine, we can make an important decision for ourselves and radically change our lives

See the beauty of the world and enjoy your vacation.

When a person becomes a tourist, he begins to explore the world not from the TV picture, but through himself. He can communicate with other peoples, see their culture, taste food, listen to colorful songs and enjoy the sights. This makes it easier to compare different points of view, to become more tolerant and friendly.

For a tourist, every new trip is like a small life. In it you can allow yourself to become different, try something new and even overcome your fears. It is also an incredible experience that affects us.

Travel is fun and always a great adventyre.

If you are going abroad not on a hot package to some all-inclusive hotel for a week, but do everything yourself, including creating an entertainment program, then adventure will definitely find you. It has been tested more than once, especially when you suddenly deviate from the plan of your program, choose a different route from the usual one, or, at worst, decide to go to some crowded cafe on the outskirts of the city. Life just has to provide something like that! Traveling is always fun! Or you bargain at the local market for some little thing and then, having snatched this very little thing for mere pennies, you feel like a hero and smile from ear to ear all day. Or you went to a restaurant to try khinkali, and there was a Georgian ensemble singing at the top of their lungs - and your mood improved! Or there are boys playing football in the yard, and as soon as they see you, they run up to say hello and shake hands. Or when you meet many different pets on your way in the mountains, you immediately run to cuddle and pet them (not to mention the million photos on your phone).

You can learn a language quickly.

Every person should know at least basic things in English so that they can communicate at least at the level of a first-grader. But since language learning at home proceeds in a passive form, when traveling you can’t go anywhere without active practice. If you communicate every day among native speakers or at least those who are the same as you, but when communicating in your native languages you do not understand each other, then the awkwardness and embarrassment gradually goes away, the level of the language improves and there are fewer misunderstandings with other people. Better than sitting at home and knowing the language only in theory.

Only on vacation can we completely relax. And we return to our everyday life as a completely different person - bolder, more interesting and open. However, there is no need to talk about this, you need to try it. Only then will you be able to appreciate the beauty of travel.
